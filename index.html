About Our Farm

Semban Thennai is a premium coconut cultivation and supply enterprise committed to delivering consistent quality to both individual customers and bulk buyers. We proudly serve Tamil Nadu and surrounding regions with reliable farm-direct supply, ensuring freshness, purity, and trust in every product.

Our farming practices emphasize natural cultivation, disciplined harvesting cycles, and strict quality control. This ensures that every coconut, sapling, and oil product meets high standards before reaching our customers.
