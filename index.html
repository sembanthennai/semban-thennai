    Our Story

    Grown with Care at Semban Thennai

    Semban Thennai stands for purity, sustainability, and trust. Rooted in the fertile lands of Barur, our farm follows traditional agricultural practices combined with modern care to ensure the highest quality coconut cultivation.

    Our focus is on producing naturally grown coconuts, cold-pressed coconut oil, and healthy saplings without the use of harmful chemicals. Every stage of our process — from cultivation to harvesting — is handled with attention to quality and respect for nature.

    We take pride in delivering farm-fresh products directly to our customers, ensuring authenticity, freshness, and long-term trust. At Semban Thennai, we are not just growing coconuts — we are nurturing a tradition of excellence.
